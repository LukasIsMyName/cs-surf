HNS ( Hide n Seek )

Hide n Seek (HNS) is quite literally the gamemode as it defines it in the title - you hide and somebody is seeking ( to kill you unfortunately ).
This gamemode is all about movement skill and does not require to have any experience in using guns, just your mouse and keyboard to escape, or - kill ( in game! ).
